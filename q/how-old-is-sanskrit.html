How Old is Sanskrit?

Classical Sanskrit was standardized by Pāṇini around the 4th century BCE, but Vedic Sanskrit predates it by many centuries. Some hymns may date back over 3000 years or more.

Sanskrit remains a living classical language with a vast body of literature, philosophy, and scientific thought.
