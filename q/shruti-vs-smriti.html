Shruti vs Smriti

Shruti refers to revealed texts such as the Vedas, considered timeless and authorless. Smriti refers to texts composed by sages, including the Dharma Shastras, Itihasas, and Puranas.

Smritis interpret, expand, and apply Shruti to social and practical life.
