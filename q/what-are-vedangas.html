What are the Vedangas?

The six Vedangas are auxiliary disciplines essential for understanding, interpreting, and practicing the Vedas:

  • Śikṣā — phonetics
  • Kalpa — ritual procedure
  • Vyākaraṇa — grammar
  • Nirukta — etymology
  • Chandas — prosody
  • Jyotiṣa — astronomy/astrology
