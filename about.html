About Swadharma

Swadharma is a non-commercial knowledge portal dedicated to preserving and explaining the scriptural, linguistic, and cultural heritage of India. Our commitment is to:

  • Scholarly accuracy
  • Neutral, respectful explanation
  • Public accessibility
  • Authentic interpretation of Dharma traditions

How Swadharma Works

  • Questions submitted by the public (anonymous allowed)
  • Draft answers generated or compiled by editors
  • Refinement and verification by scholars
  • Final pages published as static HTML for long-term access
