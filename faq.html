Frequently Asked Questions

Who may submit questions?

Anyone. You may submit anonymously or share your details for follow-up.

Who answers the questions?

Volunteers, scholars, and editors collaborate to prepare and verify answers.

Are answers AI-generated?

Editors may use AI for drafting, but final approval is always human.

Is the site free?

Yes. Swadharma is a public-education initiative. No fees or subscriptions.

Can I share the pages on social media?

Yes. You may freely share links to any Q&A page.
